Double-click an enemy to select it as a target and see its stats.

You can move your character with the W, A, S, and D keys.

To swap selected characters, either double-click or press R.

Pressing "End Turn" starts the enemy's turn.

Pressing "Attack" allows your currently selected character to attack the currently selected enemy (if in range!).
